# Qiskit-Global-Summer-School-2025-Lab-Exercise-Solutions
This repository contains my personal solutions, notes, and extended experiments from the **Qiskit Global Summer School 2025**.  
The original course materials are © IBM / Qiskit and are **not included** here.  

---

## Labs & Topics Covered

### Lab 1 — Superposition, Interference & Measurement
- Double-slit experiment simulation (superposition + interference)
- Schrödinger’s Cat revisited as a quantum state
- Quantum entanglement demo
- **CHSH Game**: Alice, Bob & the unbeatable quantum strategy
- **Quantum teleportation** (ideal + simulated)
- Teleportation circuit execution on real backend simulators

---

### Lab 2 — Quantum Noise & QAOA
- Understanding and visualizing quantum noise sources
- Automatic backend analysis: optimal T₁, T₂, gate fidelities & readout errors
- Max-Cut problem → Ising Hamiltonian → QAOA implementation
- Noisy circuit execution and backend selection strategies
- Error-mitigation pipeline using **NEAT**
- Transpiler optimization: minimizing 2-qubit gates & layout search
- Global & local **Zero Noise Extrapolation (ZNE)** demonstrations

---

### Lab 3 — Quantum Chemistry (VQE & SQD)
- Schrödinger equation & basis set approximation overview
- Hamiltonian construction & observable measurement
- **Variational Quantum Eigensolver (VQE)** implementation
- **Sample-based Quantum Diagonalization (SQD)** and noise-aware recovery
- Molecular energy simulation (H₂, LiH, etc.)
- Ansatz improvements: basis change, hardware-efficient layouts, LUCJ ansatz extensions

---

### Lab 4 — Quantum Error Correction
- Classical codes recap: parity bits, Hamming distance
- Stabilizer formalism for quantum codes
- 3-qubit bit-flip code & [[7,1,3]] Steane code walkthrough
- CSS codes and lookup-table error detection
- Advanced QEC: **Toric code**, **Gross code**
- Logical qubit counting, stabilizers, and connectivity analysis
